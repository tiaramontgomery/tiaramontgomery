VESL

The Virtual Experiences Simulation Lab (VESL) is an interdisciplinary hub where students collaborate on immersive media projects, gaining hands-on experience in areas like game development, XR, and UI/UX design. I joined the VESL in September 2023 to contribute to the data visualization efforts for the 305 360 project. As part of this initiative, I designed innovative 2D mockups representing 3D data visualizations of Miami’s dynamic landscape.

CurioCity

CurioCity is an ultimate guide to exploring new cities, providing recommendations, itineraries, navigation, and reviews to help make the most out of your visit, all within our user-friendly app. Our objective is to address the confusion and stress that comes when visiting an unfamiliar city.

Style It

Style It is a clothing app design to combat the controversial issue of feeling like you have nothing to wear in your closet full of clothes. The app will allow you to upload photos of your clothes to your wardrobe and select the items you would like to style. It will provide different styled outfit for you to choose from and to try on. The try on feature will allow you to stand in front of your phone and simulate what the outfit would look like on you.

How To Website

A How-to website designed to teach you how to gym equipment and allow you to create your own workout plan.
