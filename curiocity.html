CurioCity

Overview

Team Project · Aug 2023 – Dec 2023

CurioCity is an ultimate guide to exploring new cities, providing recommendations, itineraries, navigation, and reviews to help make the most out of your visit, all within our user-friendly app. Our objective is to address the confusion and stress that comes when visiting an unfamiliar city.

This project helped me to think both critically and creatively—deepening my understanding of user-centered design and strengthening my ability to solve problems.

Roles & Responsibility

  • This project was a 4 person group project for a class. Equal amount of work was divided amongst us.
  • We were tasked with building out only one user task flow. Our user task is for them to sign in, search for restaurants, book a reservation, and add it to their itinerary.

Goals

Differentiation Strategy

One app does it all

After conducting a comprehensive SWOT analysis of leading travel apps, we discovered that there is no single app that combines all of their features, so we decided to use the best features and implement them into one platform, ensuring users can fulfill all of their needs in a single user-friendly application.

User-Personas

College Student Persona

Problem

  • Getting to know their college town/city beyond their campus
  • Personalized recommendations for teens/people in their 20s

Solution Statement

  • Curating their interests in a personalized onboarding process to provide specific activities, sights, restaurant, stores, concerts, ect for their needs

How might we

  • Help students get to know their college town
  • Find affordable dining, experiences, activites, and entertainment
  • Provide a personalized feed for the user
  • Show a curated itinerary based on preferences

Ideation with scenarios

  • Personalized onboarding
  • Recommended itineraries
  • Personalized itineraries
  • Login page
  • Login with email
  • Enter password below email
  • Sign in directs you to home screen

Millennial Persona

Problem

  • How to effectively plan trips, see the cultural side of a city, and find local attractions/events

Solution Statement

  • Provide users with itineraries or suggested itineraries, local hidden gems, and guided reviews

How might we

  • Help make reviews more specific
  • Help them find hidden gems and not only popular attractions
  • Provide travelers with a one stop shop app experience
  • Help users plan their trips based on their preferences

Ideation with scenarios

  • Search bar for categories
  • Categorized restaurants/events
  • Filters for categories
  • Edit button for itineraries
  • Set specific requirements for the trip
  • View reviews/photos for activities
  • Share options
  • Cancellation availability

International Student Persona

Problem

  • How to help travelers see local, authentic spots, while giving them all information surrounding age restrictions, hidden fees, and realistic images of the customer experience.

Solution Statement

  • Create space for users to input specifc desires for trips and provide area for their age and more so that all results will be filtered options.

How might we

  • Help travelers to see the most local,authentic locations
  • Provide travelers with all information about age restrictions, hidden fees, and images of realistic customer experiences

Ideation with scenarios

  • Specific filters
  • Extra information button
  • Emphasize restrictions
  • Page with in depth account of all fees
  • Review page with both written responses and real life photos
  • Include price ranges
  • Buy event tickets
  • Make reservations

Usability Testing

  • User experience difficulty finding the recently added event to the itinerary.
  • Users experienced confusions with if they booked a reservation or just added it to the itinerary.
  • Users experienced confusions with what their task was of searching for a restaurant.
  • Users questioned where are the prices for menu items.
  • Users struggled to read certain words on the wire-fram due to text size.
  • Users liked the color scheme and visual design

Future Recommendations

Going forward I would love to finish building out this idea:

  • Allow users buy tickets within the app

  • Include everything in the app such as hotels, flights, and rental cars.
  • Interactions with other users like a social media app, follow other users on the app, create their own recommendation page
